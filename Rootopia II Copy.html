Terra the Turnip

Equality was gone, family members were gone, hope was gone. Terra of the Turnip race was mentally lost, she had recently graduated from Rootine's High School and was excited to embark on her new journey at Umber Rootiversity, where she was supposed to study geology. One day, she had left her house to purchase school merchandise, along with red and white paint for her room. Terra had left during the morning and returned in the evening to find that her parents were beaten to death. Traumatized by the situation, Terra had broken into tears, screaming at the world in disbelief. The television had been on and a live news broadcasting was airing information about Rachel of the Radishian's hunger for power. That day, Terra swore to avenge her parents, abandoning her future rootiversity plans.
