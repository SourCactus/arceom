Rootopia

Origin

Once long ago, peace and equality embodied the land of Rootopia, home to the many Root races. The continent was one of seven that inhabited planet earth, each with different cultures and lifestyles. The season of storms had begun and the residents of Rootopia would no longer live peaceful lives, due to a salt-infused thunderbolt striking a Root. Corruption had flowed through this particular Root, causing a desire for destruction. The Root became dehydrated over time but hungry for power over all Root races. The land of Rootopia would undergo a war lasting one hundred years.

Origin II

The Radish race was the first to be influenced by the corrupted Root, leading them to power over the other Root races during the war. A Radish by the name of Rachel, was the heir to the Radishians, one of the royal Root families of Rootopia. Rachel was a 19-year-old Radishian that discovered the feeling of bloodlust, eventually killing many of the other Root races. The Turnip were her favorite to dispose of and the lives of the residents of Rootopia had begun to diminish, peace was lost.
